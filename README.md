# Socratic Questioning for Agile Coaches

An interactive coaching companion for agile coaches who use Socratic questioning with product teams. Pull it up on your tablet or laptop during sessions.

## What's in it

- **6 Socratic Question Types** — expandable cards with agile-specific examples, timing guidance, and coaching tips
- **Question Deck** — draw random Socratic questions, filter by type, shuffle the deck. Great for preparation or when stuck mid-session
- **Situation Suggester** — floating button opens a quick-access panel. Tap "Loudest voice dominating" or "Team avoiding a decision" and get tailored questions instantly
- **Ceremony Quick-Filter** — select Sprint Planning, Refinement, Review, Retro, Daily, or 1-on-1 to see relevant questions across all types
- **Circular Question Generator** — pick two team roles and get contextual questions that surface hidden team dynamics
- **Session Journal** — log which questions you used, what happened, and track your coaching patterns over time (stored in browser localStorage)
- **Coaching Scenarios** — 6 common agile situations (defensive developer, scope argument, unrealistic deadline, quiet team member, architecture conflict, stakeholder vs. team) with phased question approaches, tips, and traps to avoid
- **Cognitive Restructuring Worksheet** — interactive fillable form for 1-on-1 coaching sessions
- **Practical Flow Guide** — visual conversation flow mapping question types to coaching phases

## Usage

Open `index.html` in a browser. No build step, no dependencies, fully offline.

All journal data stays in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server.

## Background

Inspired by:
- The six types of Socratic questions (conceptual clarification, probing assumptions, probing rationale/evidence, questioning viewpoints, probing implications, questions about the question)
- Cognitive restructuring techniques from therapeutic coaching
- Circular/systemic questioning from team coaching research
- Agile coaching practices for product teams
